Guitar App

I've always been a huge, huge fan of guitars and in my programming, I would like to develop a sort of guide, in its simplest forms, to the most iconic guitars, and who has played them. If you're a fan of Eric Clapton or Mark Knopfler, I'll point you to the Fender Stratocaster. Fan of Slash? The Gibson Les Paul, and so on. This would be a fun project for me, and hopefully an even more enjoyable clickthrough for anyone who takes a look!

Concert App

Another thing that's always been a big thing for me is concerts. From live music to live comedy to live sports, you name it. It's fun and exciting, but there's always the headache of navigating a new town, a new venue, parking lots, things to do around the venue, etc. I'd like to develop a site that doesn't just connect you to the ticket booth, but also to the planning facilitator. A real one-stop shop!
